About me

Currently, I am a postdoctoral scholar in the Department of Earth, Atmospheric, and Planetary Sciences, Purdue University.

Before that, I got my B.Sc. degree in the School of Information Engineering in June 2013 from Chang'an University. In 2015, I was admitted to study for a M.Sc degree in Xi'an Jiaotong University. Following that, I obtained master degree and PhD degree in 2018 and 2022, respectively.

Research Interests: Neural Networks, Tensor Decomposition, Seismic Data Processing, Time-frequency analysis.
